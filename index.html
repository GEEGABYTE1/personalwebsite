Studying ECE and Robotics at the University of Toronto.

Currently, my work is in World-Model based control for Hidden-State Dynamical Systems. The goal is to use RL and Planning Methods for safe and robust decision-making in physical systems.

I am developing AI for the Canadarm3 and the Human-Rated Moon Rover part of the Lunar Dawn Mission at MDA Space. Previously, I was a software lead at UTAT Rocketry leading the development of the ground station and avionics of the team's first Liquid Rocket.
